TIKI TOPPLE

How to Play

Objective: Be the first to bring your Tiki to position 1.

On your turn, choose an action:

  • Move: Go forward 1 or 2 spaces.
  • Topple: Push all tikis ahead of you back 1 space, and jump to the front.
  • Switch: Swap positions with another player.
